**Bot commands:**
- *!help* -- Post this
- *!card* -- Posts image and oracle text of a card found by name.
- *!rule* -- Posts a rule. Search either by keyword (i.e. Flying, Extort, etc.) or number (i.e. 200, 702.1, etc.)
- *!checkban* -- Send the bot a deck in DMs with the caption *!checkban*, and it will respond with a list of banned cards in it by format.
- *!roll* `XdY[dk|lh]Z+N` -- roll dice with that format.
- convert -- If you send the bot a .cod, it will automatically convert it to a .txt
